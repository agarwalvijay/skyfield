Hyperlocal NWS forecasts with live radar, rain timing, alerts & widgets


FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)
=================================
Skyfield is a clean, fast weather app built directly on the United States National Weather Service — the same official forecasts and warnings meteorologists rely on, with no proprietary "blended" data in between.

Your forecast is hyperlocal: Skyfield reads your exact GPS coordinates, not just the nearest city, so what you see is the weather where you actually are.

WHAT YOU GET

• Current conditions — temperature, feels-like, humidity, wind, and sky conditions from the nearest reporting station.
• Hour-by-hour forecast — see exactly how the day unfolds.
• 7-day forecast — highs, lows, and conditions at a glance.
• Minute-by-minute rain — a short-term "next 2 hours" nowcast tells you when rain or snow is about to start or stop.
• Live radar — animated precipitation radar you can scrub through time, centered on your location.
• Severe weather alerts — official NWS watches and warnings, front and center, with optional push notifications.
• Rain notifications — a heads-up the moment rain is starting or stopping near you.
• Forecaster Discussion — read the meteorologist's own full reasoning (the raw Area Forecast Discussion) when you want the why behind the forecast.

HOME-SCREEN WIDGETS
Add a banner or square widget for current conditions, hi/lo, wind, humidity, and active alerts at a glance — refreshed automatically, with a tap-to-refresh button.

MADE TO BE SIMPLE
• No account required.
• Choose your units — Fahrenheit/Celsius, mph / km/h / m/s / knots.
• Track your current location, or pin saved places.

DATA & ATTRIBUTION

Coverage is currently the United States and its territories (the areas served by the NWS).
